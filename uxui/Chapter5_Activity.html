Class Dilemma Poll
A designer's goal: should you always maximize social presence in your product?

Click an option to cast your vote — students can vote individually on their devices.

Instructor context — after revealing votes:

There is no universally correct answer. Use the split in the room to probe: "Who answered Depends — what condition would tip you to Yes?" Connect to the chapter: higher presence isn't always better (e.g., anonymous support groups benefit from reduced presence). The question is always: presence for whom, doing what?

Follow-up Scenario
Eugenia Kuyda built a chatbot trained on her deceased friend's texts so she could keep talking to him.

As the UX designer of this product — what ONE design decision would you make to ensure it is ethical?

Real products that have tried this:

Replika — AI companion app. HereAfterAI — lets people record memories before death. StoryFile — interactive video chatbot of deceased. Ask students: have any of these companies solved the ethical challenges the class raised?
