Program for NSF CISE-MSI Aspiring PI Workshop

The University of North Texas (UNT) is hosting the NSF CISE MSI Aspiring PI Workshop at Denton Convention Center, Denton, Texas on Nov 22-23, 2024.

Day 1:
8:00am -9:00 am: Breakfast
9:00am -9:15am: Opening
9:15am-10:15am:  Informational Session (Training: Introduction to NSF Program Opportunities for MSIs by NSF Program Directors)
10:15am-10:30am: Break  
10:30am-12:00pm: Breakout Session I (Research partnerships: Research topic-based breakout session – Understanding potential partner’s research interest and expertise. Each participant will engage with at least two topic groups, rotating every 30 minutes)
12:00pm-1:30pm: Lunch and networking (Research partnerships: Informal networking opportunities with topic-specific table settings
1:30pm-2:30pm:  Informational Session (Training: Emphasizing Intellectual Merit (IM) and Broad Impact (BI) criteria, and strategies for identifying and presenting high-quality IM & BI by NSF Program Directors)
2:30pm-2:45pm: Break
2:45pm-4:15pm:  Breakout Session II (Research partnership: Brainstorming with potential collaborators identified in Breakout Session I and during Lunch)
4:15pm-4:30pm:  Break
4:30pm-5:00pm:  Breakout Session Report (Research partnership: Presenting proposal team information: tentative team members, research topics, etc.)
5:00pm-6:00pm: Poster Session and networking (Research partnership)
6:00-8:00pm:  Dinner and Proposal Team Finalization (Research partnership: Introducing Mentors, Proposal team submitting team information)       


Day 2:
8:00am -9:00 am: Breakfast
9:00am-10:00am: Informational Session (Training: Keynote on structuring and writing high-quality NSF proposals by invited keynote speaker)
10:00am-10:15am: Break  
10:15am-11:45am: Breakout Session III ( Research Partnership : proposal team meeting with mentors to discuss research proposal ideas and develop the proposal preparation plans)
11:45am-12:15pm: Breakout Session Report (Research partnership: present of team information, topic, and plan)
12:15pm-1:30pm: Lunch
1:30pm-3:30pm: Breakout Proposal Working Session (Research partnership: Proposal team refining project ideas and proposal writing agenda, identifying the tasks prior to the second wor kshop)
3:30pm-4:00pm Closing remark and announcement of deliverables for the next workshop

Virtual workshop agenda:
Day 1:


9:00am -9:15am:  Opening
9:15am-10:15am:  Informational Session (Training: Introduction to proposal review procedures and tips of preparing proposal from reviewer’s perspective by NSF Program Directors)
10:15am-10:30am: Break  
10:30am-12:00pm:  Progress Report Session (Research partnership: Progress report on working proposal: Progress and Challenges)
12:00pm-1:30pm:    Lunch Break
1:30pm-2:30pm:  Informational Session (Training: Preparing Budget for NSF proposals by NSF Program Directors)
2:30pm-2:45pm: Break
2:45pm-4:15pm:  Breakout Session II (Research partnership: Mentor and Team Working Session: mentor’s feedback on proposal progress and advice on addressing challenges)
4:15pm-4:30pm:  Break
4:30pm-5:00pm:  Breakout Session Report (Sharing the feedback and advice from mentors)        

Day 2:


9:00am-10:00am: Breakout Sessions with NSF POs (Training: Q&A Session with NSF POs in topic-specific virtual rooms: NSF programs, review process, budget, etc.)
10:00am-10:15am: Break  
10:15am-11:30 am: Breakout Proposal Working Session with Mentors (Research partnership: Proposal team and mentor collaboration on proposal submission planning)
11:30am-12:00pm: Breakout Session Report (Present proposal submission plan)
