Registration & Travel

The University of North Texas (UNT) is hosting the NSF CISE MSI Aspiring PI Workshop at Denton Convention Center, Denton, Texas on Nov 22-23, 2024.

If you would like to attend please complete this registration form. If you did not receive an invitation please contact us with your interest in attending. Participants will receive a stipend to cover or defray the cost of travel, meals, and lodging.

After registering you will receive instructions via email about how to arrange for travel and reimbursement for expenses. Travelers flying in should arrive at the Dallas DFW Airport.

The workshop will be held at a Location Near University of North Texas at Denton Convention Center, Address: 3100 Town Center Tr, Denton, TX 76201 (Embassy Suites by Hilton Denton Convention Center)
